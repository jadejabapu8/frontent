Introducing Astrite Linery is an elegant serif font combined with a handwritten script font that will add elegance and style to your designs. This duo font delivers a classy and elegant aesthetic, making it perfect for headlines, logos, covers, posters, quotes, magazines, wedding invitations, product packaging, social media, marketing materials, and more.

FEATURE :
- TTF/OTF
- WOFF/WOFF2
- Ligature
- Uppercase and lowercase
- Numbering and Punctuation
- Works on PC or Mac
- Simple Installation
- Supports Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, also works in Microsoft Word
