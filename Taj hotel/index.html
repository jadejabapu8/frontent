___EXQUISITE
ARTISINAL
INDULGENCE


Spread over six acres, Taj Gandhinagar Resort & Spais a sanctuary of serenity and indulgence, offering a tranquil retreat with wellness offerings to rejuvenate your mind, body and soul. A short drive from the airport leads you to winding pathways sheltered by soaring trees and millet farms, setting the stage for a sensorial delight. The charm of a Mediterranean estate is evoked by distinctive blue domes, stately pillars, and arches that welcome you into the lobby adorned with stunning paintings, grand chandeliers, and curated artefacts. Our well-appointed rooms and suites are meticulously designed for maximum comfort and relaxation, featuring tasteful furniture, rich textiles, French windows with lush green views, select artefacts and spacious bathrooms to ensure a splendid stay. Indulgence awaits at Taj Gandhinagar Resort & Spa, where our chefs craft world cuisine and local favourites at Palette, serve delicious blends and Ayurvedic infusions at Tea Lounge, and offer an organic plant-based menu at the J Wellness Café , besides offering 24-hour in-room dining. Our hotel in Gandhinagar offers holistic wellness spa facilities that provide ultimate pampering, with various fitness options to burn calories and a space designed for comprehensive rejuvenation.
