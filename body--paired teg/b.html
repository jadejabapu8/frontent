Ravindra Jadeja is an Indian cricketer, born on December 6, 1988, in Jamnagar, Gujarat. He is a left-handed batsman and a left-arm orthodox bowler, known for his all-round abilities. Jadeja has played for the Indian national team in all formats of the game, as well as for the Chennai Super Kings in the IPL.
