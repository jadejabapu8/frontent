Jesal Jadeja

Jesal Jadeja was a 14th-century dacoit (bandit) from Kutch, Gujarat, who was later revered as a saint. He is known for his transformation after encountering the saint-poetess Toral. Their story is a popular folk legend in Gujarat, particularly in Kutch, and their samadhi (tomb) is a place of pilgrimage.
