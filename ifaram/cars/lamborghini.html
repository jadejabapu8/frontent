lamborghini

    Lamborghini, founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer known for its high-performance vehicles. The company, now owned by the Volkswagen Group through Audi, is headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. Lamborghini's model lineup includes the Huracan, Urus, and Revuelto, with prices ranging from ₹4.57 Crore to ₹8.89 Crore.
