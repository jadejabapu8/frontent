defender

    The Land Rover Defender is a rugged, off-road capable SUV known for its versatility and technological advancements. It comes in various configurations, including 90 (3-door) and 110 (5-door) body styles, and offers multiple engine options, including petrol and diesel, some with mild hybrid technology. The Defender boasts a strong focus on both capability and personalization, offering a wide range of accessories and customization options.
